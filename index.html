Frequently Asked Questions

How do AI agents work in the office?

Each AI agent is an avatar with assignable skills. You can extend its abilities via our AI Skill Marketplace without creating a new agent.

Do I need to install anything?

No—our workspace runs entirely in the browser. Simply sign up, invite teammates, and start collaborating.

Can I integrate my existing tools?

Yes. We offer one-click integrations with Slack, Notion, Loom, GitHub, and more—so you keep using what you love.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We provide end-to-end encryption, granular permissions, and SOC 2 compliance to keep your information safe.
